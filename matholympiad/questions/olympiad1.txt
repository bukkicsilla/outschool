Olympiad 1

1. Problem:
Suppose today is Tuesday. What day of the week will it be 30 days from now?

2. Problem:
I have three 3¢-stamps and two 5¢-stamps. Using one or more of these stamps,
how many different amounts of postage can I make?

3. Problem:
Find the sum of the counting numbers from 1 to 15 inclusive. In order words,
if S = 1 + 2 + 3 + ... + 14 + 15, find the value of S.

4. Problem:
In a stationary store, pencils have one price and pens have another price.
One pencil and two pens cost 26¢. But two pencils and one pen cost 28¢.
How much does one pen cost?

5. Problem:
A work crew of 2 people requires 10 days to do a certain job. How long
would it take a work crew of 5 people to do the same job if each person of
both crews works at the same rate as each of the others?
