1. Problem:
A girl bought a dog for $10, sold it for $15, bought it back for $20,
and finally sold it for $25. Did the girl make or lose money,
and how much did she make or lose?

2. Problem:
I have 30 coins consisting of nickels and quarters. The total value of the coins is $4.10.
How many of each kind do I have?

3. Problem:
Rectangular cards, 2 inches by 3 inches, are cut from a rectangular sheet 2 feet by
3 feet. What is the greatest number of cards that can be cut from the sheet?

4. Problem:
In three bowling games, Alice scores 139, 143, and 144. What score
will Alice need in a fourth game in order to have an average score
of 145 for all four games?

5. Problem:
A book has 500 pages numbered 1, 2, 3, and so on. How many times does
the digit 1 appear in the page numbers?
